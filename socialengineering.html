🎭 Social Engineering

Learn about psychological manipulation techniques used by attackers to trick users into revealing confidential information.

📱 Smishing

SMS-based phishing attacks that trick users into clicking malicious links or sharing sensitive information.

📞 Vishing

Voice phishing attacks where criminals impersonate banks or officials over phone calls.

🎣 Baiting

Attackers lure victims using attractive offers such as free software, USB drives or rewards.

🎭 Pretexting

Creating a fake identity or believable story to convince victims to reveal confidential information.

🚪 Tailgating

Unauthorized individuals gain physical access to restricted areas by following authorized people.

👀 Shoulder Surfing

Stealing passwords or confidential information by secretly watching someone enter them.

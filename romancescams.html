❤️ Romance Scams

🏷 Modern Threats 🟢 Beginner ⏱ 2–3 min read

What are Romance Scams?

Romance scams are cyber frauds where criminals create fake online identities on dating apps or social media to build emotional relationships with victims. After gaining their trust, the scammers manipulate victims into sending money, gifts, or sensitive personal information.

How Does It Work?

  1. The scammer creates a fake profile using attractive photos and false information.
  2. They spend weeks or months building trust through regular conversations.
  3. They avoid meeting in person or making video calls.
  4. Eventually, they invent an emergency and ask for money or financial assistance.

Real-Life Example

A person meets someone on a dating platform who claims to work overseas. After several weeks of chatting, the scammer says they need money for a medical emergency or travel expenses. The victim transfers the money, only to discover later that the profile was fake.

Warning Signs

  • The relationship progresses unusually fast.
  • The person refuses video calls or face-to-face meetings.
  • They ask for money or expensive gifts.
  • They create emotional stories to gain sympathy.
  • They claim to be working abroad or in the military.
  • Their profile pictures appear too perfect or stolen.

How to Stay Safe

  • Never send money to someone you have only met online.
  • Verify identities through video calls.
  • Use reverse image search to check profile pictures.
  • Discuss suspicious requests with trusted friends or family.
  • Keep personal and financial information private.
  • Report suspicious profiles to the platform.

What to Do If You Are a Victim

  • Stop all communication immediately.
  • Do not send any more money.
  • Save chats, emails, and payment records as evidence.
  • Contact your bank if money was transferred.
  • Report the incident to cybercrime authorities.
  • Warn friends and family about the scam.

💡 Quick Tips

  • True relationships are built on trust, not financial requests.
  • Be cautious if someone expresses love very quickly.
  • Always verify before sending money online.

🤔 Did You Know?

Romance scams cause millions of dollars in losses every year because scammers exploit human emotions and trust rather than technical vulnerabilities.

Common Attacks

Understand the most common cyber attacks and learn how to protect yourself.

🎣 Phishing

Fraudulent emails or messages designed to steal passwords and personal information.



🦠 Malware

Malicious software that damages systems or steals sensitive information.



🔒 Ransomware

Encrypts files and demands payment to restore access.



🐴 Trojan Horse

Appears harmless but secretly installs malicious software.



👁️ Spyware

Secretly monitors user activities and steals confidential information.



🪱 Worm

Self-replicating malware that spreads automatically through networks.



🌐 DDoS Attack

Overloads servers with traffic and disrupts services.
